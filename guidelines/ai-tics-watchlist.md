prune them during editing. Each entry lists its inflected forms in parentheses.

Source: the Novelcrafter codex ("other" entries tagged as annoyances), archived
under `source/raw/2026-06-23-novelcrafter-export/other/`. This supplements the
"Avoid these words and their variations" line in `guidelines.md`; the list keeps
expanding, so treat it as living.
