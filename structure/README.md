# structure/

Working folder for **structural** analysis of the manuscript — the layer between
line-editing (`guidelines/`) and pure continuity dates (`timeline/`).

This is where we keep audits that ask the bigger questions:

- Do the characters stay true to how they're described and to what the craft
  notes say we're *trying* to do with them?
- Do the events make sense, and where is the world least plausible?
- What are the weakest, most inconsistent beats — the things a careful reader
  would snag on?

It is **not** a line-edit log and **not** a date-by-date continuity log. For
those, see `guidelines/reports/` and `timeline/`.

## Contents

| File | What it is |
|---|---|
| `book1-audit.md` | Full interrogation of Book 1: character fidelity, event plausibility, and a ranked list of the weakest/most inconsistent things. (Sibling finding amended after the Book 2 read — see its header note.) |
| `book2-audit.md` | Same treatment for Book 2, plus a **cross-book reconciliation** section (siblings, office floor, employment history) that resolves what Book 1 left ambiguous. |
