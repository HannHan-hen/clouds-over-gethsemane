29. Average height, pretty in a soft way. Pale complexion, short black hair, large, gray eyes. Tends to dress simply in plain shirts, basic suits. Favors blending in over standing out. Introverted, meek, awkward, and shy, especially in high-stakes social environments. He's not naive about the world but chooses not to participate in its more cynical games. He has low self-esteem and is a loner. Grew up in a small town, attended a mediocre university, moved to the city for work. His first job ended in trauma when his boss sexually harassed him, leaving him with lasting mistrust. Never knew his father; his mother remarried and had other children, leading to dwindling contact with his family.
