38. CFO of a prestigious investment fund. Tall, lean, always carefully styled hair, blue eyes. Angular, aristocratic features. Dresses impeccably in expensive suits. Always composed in public. Maintains perfect posture. Rarely smiles. Speaks clearly, rarely wastes words. Firstborn of an affluent family. Ostracized for being openly gay, which led to him being shunned and removed from his position as heir, but clawed his way to prominence through talent and discipline. Once fell for a subordinate, causing scandal and permanent wariness about mixing love and work.
