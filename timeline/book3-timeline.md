# Book 3 Timeline: Clouds Over Gethsemane

## Starting Point
Book 3 begins on Thursday, August 1, 2019 -- Jay's two-year anniversary at Turner & Associates. Book 2 ended in late July 2019 with Jay processing the Gal incident and Ian's embrace.

---

## Chapter 41: New traditions

### Scene 1: Companions
- **Duration:** 1 day
- **Date:** Thursday, August 1, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Thursday
- **Summary:** Jay marks his two-year anniversary at Turner & Associates, reflecting on how far he's come. He and Ian have a normal briefing, then share a quiet dinner at a cozy pasta restaurant where Ian thanks Jay for staying.
- **Milestone:** Jay's two-year work anniversary; first dinner together "as companions, not boss and assistant"

### Scene 2: Obvious favouritism
- **Duration:** 1 day
- **Date:** Saturday, August 3, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Saturday
- **Summary:** Jay tells Rus about the Gal situation at the gym, receiving emotional support. Afterwards, he endures his tailor appointment for Leo's birthday gift. Leo arrives at his apartment to discuss planning Ian's 40th birthday party.

### Scene 3: Vanilla and spices
- **Duration:** 1 week
- **Date:** Monday, August 5, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Monday
- **Summary:** Jay broaches the birthday party topic with Ian, who reluctantly agrees. Jay begins making homemade tinctures as Ian's birthday gift, ordering supplies and converting his kitchen into a distillery. Ian starts driving Jay home regularly and they establish Friday evening dinners.
- **Milestone:** Ian agrees to birthday party; Jay begins making tinctures; Friday dinners become tradition

### Scene 4: Safe love
- **Duration:** 1 week
- **Date:** Monday, August 12, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Monday
- **Summary:** Jay reflects on his feelings for Ian, accepting his love as unrequited but finding peace in it. He identifies as bisexual. They establish Tuesday evening runs together as a new routine.
- **Milestone:** Jay accepts his love for Ian and his bisexuality; Tuesday runs established

### Scene 5: Running
- **Duration:** 1 week
- **Date:** Tuesday, August 20, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Tuesday
- **Summary:** Jay and Ian's first run together along the river path. Jay struggles but perseveres. Ian coaches him patiently. They continue their Friday dinner tradition, their lives intertwining more deeply.
- **Milestone:** First Tuesday evening run together

## Chapter 42: Invitations

### Scene 1: Excessive is the minimum
- **Duration:** 1 week
- **Date:** Saturday, August 24, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Saturday
- **Summary:** Jay discusses Tim's 18th birthday car gift with his mother. Leo shows Jay the party invitations at his penthouse -- gold-embossed for a 500-person gala with past friends, clients, and former flames of Ian's. Jay feels increasingly anxious about both the scale and his gift.

### Scene 2: Complications
- **Duration:** 2 weeks
- **Date:** Saturday, September 7, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Saturday
- **Summary:** Rus reveals he received a party invitation. Jay and Ian attend a classical concert together, but Ian becomes distant due to memories of his ex Sam Connelly. Ema calls begging to attend the party, creating family tension. Jay can't reach Leo for two weeks.
- **Milestone:** Concert date with Ian; Ema invitation crisis begins

### Scene 3: The most proper man
- **Duration:** 2 weeks
- **Date:** Friday, September 20, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Friday
- **Summary:** Jay caves and allows Ema to attend with her friend Lam, after lengthy negotiations with his mother. He visits Hathaway's Menswear where Eli helps him choose a daring outfit for the party. Leo finally resurfaces, having traveled to three countries for Ian's gift.

### Scene 4: Tia Larson
- **Duration:** 2 weeks
- **Date:** Monday, September 2, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Monday
- **Summary:** Tia Larson starts at Turner & Associates, replacing Gal. She adapts quickly and proves direct and capable. Jay participates in her integration while maintaining appropriate caution. Tuesday runs and Friday dinners continue.
- **Milestone:** Tia Larson joins the team

### Scene 5: Daring outfit
- **Duration:** 1 week
- **Date:** Saturday, September 14, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Saturday
- **Summary:** Jay agonizes over his tinctures and Ian's gift. He finalizes his party outfit at Hathaway's -- a floral blazer with open collar. He invites Eli to the party and arranges a hairstylist appointment for the day of.

## Chapter 43: The celebration

### Scene 1: Gold lions and carved dragons
- **Duration:** 2 weeks
- **Date:** Monday, September 16, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Monday
- **Summary:** Jay visits Leo's office for Eli's invitation. He helps his mother research Tim's birthday car. The car dealership contact from Ian begins searching. Jay finalizes his tincture gift -- three crystal bottles in a wooden box with a handwritten note.

### Scene 2: Incandescent
- **Duration:** 1 week
- **Date:** Saturday, September 28, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Saturday
- **Summary:** Ian's 40th birthday party. Ema and Lam arrive Friday, get makeovers Saturday. Jay arrives late due to Leo's errands, descending the staircase looking incandescent. Ian is speechless. Jay tells Ian he matters.
- **Milestone:** Ian's 40th birthday party; Jay stuns the crowd

### Scene 3: Ian's world
- **Duration:** 1 evening (continued)
- **Date:** Saturday, September 28, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Saturday
- **Summary:** Jay navigates the 500-person gala, checking on Ema (in a modest Victorian gown) and the team. He discovers Ian's world is enormous, distinguished, and unapologetically queer. He monitors everyone's safety throughout the night.

### Scene 4: Not boyfriend
- **Duration:** 1 evening (continued)
- **Date:** Saturday, September 28, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Saturday
- **Summary:** The cake, fireworks, and vaudeville star performance cap the evening. Ian's friend mistakes Jay for his boyfriend; Jay forcefully denies being "available." Jay introduces Eli to Ian. He takes Ema and Lam home at 2 AM.

### Scene 5: You deserve every good thing
- **Duration:** 2 days
- **Date:** Sunday, September 29, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Sunday
- **Summary:** Ian opens Jay's gift -- three handmade tinctures with a heartfelt note. He's deeply moved, declaring it the best gift (tied with Ema's botanical garden tickets). Leo visits and teases Ian about his obvious feelings. Jay takes the girls shopping, then worries he ruined the party.
- **Milestone:** Ian receives Jay's handmade tinctures; Leo's brooch gift revealed

## Chapter 44: Afterglow

### Scene 1: Early darkness
- **Duration:** 1 week
- **Date:** Monday, September 30, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Monday
- **Summary:** Jay apologizes to Ian for the party's excess; Ian dismisses his guilt and reveals he opened Jay's gift first. Jay offers to help sort the mountain of birthday gifts. Tuesday runs are paused as autumn darkness falls early.
- **Milestone:** Tuesday evening runs paused until spring

### Scene 2: Thoughtful gifts
- **Duration:** 1 day
- **Date:** Saturday, October 5, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Saturday
- **Summary:** Jay helps Ian sort birthday gifts at his apartment. Highlights include matching gym hoodies from Rus, botanical garden tickets from Ema, and Leo's absurdly expensive antique brooch. Jay proposes a joint vacation; Ian agrees. They plan a November trip.
- **Milestone:** Joint vacation planned; botanical garden trip set for spring

### Scene 3: Smooth sailing
- **Duration:** 2 weeks
- **Date:** Monday, October 7, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Monday
- **Summary:** Quarterly reporting begins but proceeds at unprecedented speed thanks to Ray and Liv. The team finishes a day early with no overtime. Jay and Ian book a 10-day vacation to the Songbird Islands for November. Leo announces a hot springs trip for late October.
- **Milestone:** Fastest quarterly reporting ever; Songbird Islands vacation booked

### Scene 4: Lovesick
- **Duration:** 1 week
- **Date:** Saturday, October 12, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Saturday
- **Summary:** Jay is miserable from missed Friday dinners and paused routines. The car salesman finally finds Tim's car. Ian accompanies Jay to the dealership. Jay drives to his hometown for Tim's birthday weekend, giving him the car.
- **Milestone:** Tim's 18th birthday; car gift revealed

### Scene 5: Pining
- **Duration:** 1 weekend
- **Date:** Friday, October 18, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Friday
- **Summary:** Ian pines alone with Jay's red currant tincture while Jay is away. Jay attends Tim's birthday party in their hometown, a humble contrast to Ian's gala. He returns by train on Sunday, both he and Ian counting down to the hot springs trip.

---

## Chapter 45: Steam and stars

### Scene 1: Salt-ocean smell
- **Duration:** 1 day (travel + arrival)
- **Date:** Friday, October 25, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Friday
- **Summary:** The team flies to Frostbay Sanctuary, a cliff-side resort on the Ashland coast. Ray surprises everyone by attending. Jay and Ian share a suite. They soak in turquoise mineral pools and explore the dramatic coastal landscape.
- **Milestone:** Company hot springs trip begins; Ray voluntarily attends

### Scene 2: Aurora
- **Duration:** 1 evening
- **Date:** Friday, October 25, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Friday
- **Summary:** After a hearty dinner, the group visits the ocean-edge pool to watch the northern lights. Green, purple, and blue aurora fills the sky while they soak in steaming water with freezing ocean spray nearby. Jay calls it the most beautiful thing he's ever seen.
- **Milestone:** Aurora borealis viewing from the hot springs

### Scene 3: Very interesting wood
- **Duration:** 1 day
- **Date:** Saturday, October 26, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Saturday
- **Summary:** The team hikes in freezing conditions. Jay has to turn back due to inadequate clothing (no thermal underwear). He and Ian soak in a private pool instead, then the whole team does a traditional naked communal sauna. Jay is flustered by Ian's physique.

### Scene 4: Pitiful plea
- **Duration:** 1 evening
- **Date:** Saturday, October 26, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Saturday
- **Summary:** Jay drinks too much after the sauna. Outside in the freezing night, he clings to Ian. Back in their room, drunk and emotional, Jay whispers "I want you to hold me for the rest of my life." Ian holds him as he falls asleep, devastated by love he can't act on.
- **Milestone:** Jay's drunken confession -- "hold me for the rest of my life"

### Scene 5: Cruel joke
- **Duration:** 1 day
- **Date:** Sunday, October 27, 2019
- **Day of Week:** Sunday
- **Summary:** Jay wakes mortified with fragmented memories. Ian reassures him nothing happened. They get massages, soak once more, then fly home. Jay suspects something is wrong; Ian privately falls apart, calling Jay's words "the cruellest part of all" -- hearing exactly what he wanted from someone he can never have.
