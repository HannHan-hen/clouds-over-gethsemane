# AU Book One Plan - Xianxia Danmei (20 Chapters, 5 Scenes Each)

## Core Arc Notes
- **Tone progression:** bleak distrust -> careful cooperation -> mutual reliance -> open tenderness under pressure -> emotionally devastating restraint.
- **Relationship spine:** Ian is wary because Jay resembles the traitor who ruined his former clan life; Jay admires Ian and interprets his coldness as fair caution, never resentment.
- **The recurring wound:** Ian's early coldness never leaves him. Even after trust grows, he privately treats every kindness toward Jay as partial atonement.
- **Slice-of-life + xianxia stakes:** daily sect chores, lessons, patrols, herb prep, scripture copying, spirit-beast care, plus raids, ambushes, tournaments, cursed artifacts, and clan politics.

---

## Chapter 1 - Ashes and Gate Oaths
### Scene 1
Jay arrives at the mountain gate of **Qingsong Clan** with a probation token and worn travel robes, witnessing disciples sneer at his unknown lineage.
### Scene 2
Leo (clan leader's son) theatrically introduces Jay to inner-court administrators and insists he be assigned to Ian's division.
### Scene 3
Ian meets Jay, is instantly rattled by familiar mannerisms that echo the old traitor, and responds with formal icy distance.
### Scene 4
Jay receives work: inventory talismans, sort medicine slips, copy defensive array maps overnight; he accepts gratefully.
### Scene 5
Night patrol alarm: minor spirit breach. Jay helps evacuate junior disciples efficiently; Ian notices competence but assumes calculation.

## Chapter 2 - Cold Lessons
### Scene 1
Morning training on sword forms and breath cycles; Jay struggles with elite-level pacing but refuses to quit.
### Scene 2
Ian tests Jay with impossible filing and courier routes across floating pavilions; Jay completes all without complaint.
### Scene 3
Luc befriends Jay over canteen porridge, complains Ian is too harsh; Jay defends Ian as strict but righteous.
### Scene 4
Leo publicly scolds Ian for turning his office into an ice cave; Jay hurriedly says he truly doesn't mind.
### Scene 5
Ian catches Jay quietly mending damaged practice talismans for poorer outer disciples and feels first crack in suspicion.

## Chapter 3 - Quiet Usefulness
### Scene 1
Jay begins dawn routine: well water haul, scripture recitation, hidden self-study of cultivation theory.
### Scene 2
Council meeting crisis: missing spirit-stone ledger. Jay reconstructs records from memory and old shipping seals.
### Scene 3
Ian gives concise praise in front of seniors; Jay glows for hours though Ian's tone remains flat.
### Scene 4
A petty disciple spreads rumors Jay advanced via beauty and Leo's favor; Jay absorbs humiliation in silence.
### Scene 5
Ian overhears and coldly shuts the rumor down with official disciplinary penalties, then acts as if nothing happened.

## Chapter 4 - Echo of Treachery
### Scene 1
Jay asks about a sealed archive battle map tied to Ian's former clan; Ian freezes and cuts him off sharply.
### Scene 2
Flashback intercut: Ian's past lover-traitor opening arrays to enemy raiders, clan elders slain, Ian cast out in disgrace.
### Scene 3
Jay withdraws to minimal speech and perfect compliance, believing he overstepped and dishonored Ian's pain.
### Scene 4
Luc and Leo confront Ian; Ian insists caution is necessary, but his defense sounds hollow even to himself.
### Scene 5
At night, Ian finds Jay cleaning blood from training grounds after everyone left and feels crushing guilt for earlier harshness.

## Chapter 5 - First Hunt
### Scene 1
Jay is assigned logistics for a low-tier night-hunt in mist valleys; Ian reluctantly allows him to accompany the unit.
### Scene 2
Ambush by corpse puppets controlled through hidden flute technique; formation breaks in panic.
### Scene 3
Jay drags an injured youth to safety and stabilizes him with basic qi-thread stitches learned from common schools.
### Scene 4
Ian duels the masked controller, wins, but takes a poisoned cut shielding Jay from a surprise strike.
### Scene 5
Back at clan infirmary, Jay keeps vigil grinding antidote herbs; Ian wakes to see him asleep at bedside.

## Chapter 6 - Uneven Thaw
### Scene 1
Ian resumes work early despite poison fever; Jay enforces physician orders with respectful stubbornness.
### Scene 2
Leo teases Ian for obeying Jay; Ian denies everything while drinking the bitter tonic Jay brewed.
### Scene 3
Luc complains Ian never says thank you; Jay says Ian protected everyone and needs no applause.
### Scene 4
An accounting dispute reveals embezzled talisman paper from supply stores; Jay helps trace culprits through handwriting.
### Scene 5
Ian and Jay close case together at midnight archives, sharing first calm conversation not strictly about tasks.

## Chapter 7 - Winter Market, Hidden Knives
### Scene 1
Clan delegation travels to winter market city for spirit-metal procurement; Jay handles bargaining notes.
### Scene 2
Street cultivators mock Jay's lowborn aura signature; Ian silences them with rank pressure.
### Scene 3
Assassin disguised as tea server attacks Ian with soul-pin needle; Jay spots movement and disrupts trajectory.
### Scene 4
Chase across lantern bridges ends with Luc helping corner attacker tied to Ian's old enemies.
### Scene 5
Returning to inn, Ian gives Jay a protective talisman "for office duty only"; Jay treasures it like sacred trust.

## Chapter 8 - Snowbound Proximity
### Scene 1
Blizzard seals mountain roads; department is stranded in main hall quarters, forcing shared work and living spaces.
### Scene 2
Jay organizes rotating chores and warm congee for exhausted disciples; morale improves.
### Scene 3
Ian privately witnesses Jay giving his own fur blanket to a shivering stable hand.
### Scene 4
Late-night scroll sorting by brazier: Jay asks nothing personal, just listens while Ian speaks briefly about failure.
### Scene 5
Powerful ward tremor hits during storm; Jay and Ian reinforce charms together, hands bloodied from overchanneling qi.

## Chapter 9 - Lantern Festival Trial
### Scene 1
Sect hosts a public lantern rite to reassure nearby villages after winter spirit surges.
### Scene 2
Jay coordinates offerings, seating, security lines; Ian monitors arrays from the tower.
### Scene 3
Rival clan provocateurs release resentment wraith into crowd; panic erupts near children section.
### Scene 4
Jay lures wraith toward empty square with guiding bells while Ian seals it in thunder script.
### Scene 5
Festival saved. Elders praise Ian; Ian redirects merit to Jay in formal report, shocking everyone including Jay.

## Chapter 10 - Spring Tournament
### Scene 1
Preparation montage: weapon polishing, form correction, betting chatter; Jay enters support division contests.
### Scene 2
Leo forces Ian to judge talisman strategy bracket where Jay competes against privileged disciples.
### Scene 3
Jay wins through patience and precise qi control, not flashy power; crowd reaction shifts from ridicule to respect.
### Scene 4
A competitor accuses Jay of cheating with forbidden bloodline art; Ian publicly demands proof and clears Jay.
### Scene 5
Evening celebration turns quiet when Ian awkwardly congratulates Jay one-on-one; Jay nearly cries from happiness.

## Chapter 11 - Blood at the River Pass
### Scene 1
Emergency summons: demon boars and corrupted vines overrun River Pass caravans.
### Scene 2
Ian leads strike team with Jay as logistics-adjutant; Luc joins as spear specialist.
### Scene 3
Battle escalates when hidden formation traps civilians inside collapsing ferry shrine.
### Scene 4
Jay enters danger zone to guide survivors out; Ian breaks formation orders to cover him, taking heavy internal injury.
### Scene 5
After victory, Ian collapses. Jay carries him down mountain steps despite torn meridians.

## Chapter 12 - Fever Dreams and Watchfires
### Scene 1
Ian's injuries worsen into qi-deviation fevers; physicians prohibit most visitors.
### Scene 2
Jay bribes kitchen aunties for medicinal broths and passes notes to infirmary servants for updates.
### Scene 3
Leo catches Jay outside infirmary at dawn and finally reveals full story of Ian's betrayal and exile.
### Scene 4
Jay weeps alone, not from fear of Ian, but from grief that Ian endured so much and still protects others.
### Scene 5
Ian wakes during storm night and finds Jay at the window chanting stability sutras softly for him.

## Chapter 13 - The Poison Ledger
### Scene 1
Recovered assassin token leads to black-market apothecary ring selling meridian poison to sect enemies.
### Scene 2
Ian insists on field command before full recovery; Jay argues, then compromises on strict support protocols.
### Scene 3
Undercover visit to river slums: Jay plays quiet clerk while Ian bargains with crime broker.
### Scene 4
Trap springs; Luc is wounded by cursed chain. Jay stabilizes him while Ian destroys the poison lab.
### Scene 5
They secure ledgers listing buyers linked to the same faction that ruined Ian's birth clan.

## Chapter 14 - Rain Season Burdens
### Scene 1
Monsoon leaks damage archive hall; daily slice-of-life repair work bonds disciples and mentors.
### Scene 2
Ian teaches Jay advanced ward calculus during roof patch breaks, impressed by Jay's relentless study habits.
### Scene 3
Luc complains Ian's standards are impossible; Jay counters that Ian trains people to survive, not to flatter ego.
### Scene 4
Leo stages dramatic "intervention dinner" about Ian's coldness; Jay publicly thanks Ian for fairness and protection.
### Scene 5
Ian later admits to Leo he cannot forgive himself for first-month cruelty toward Jay.

## Chapter 15 - The Burial Mound Raid
### Scene 1
Scouts report mass corpse awakening near ancestral burial mounds; multiple clans send mixed coalition.
### Scene 2
Political tension rises as nobles question Jay's presence in strategy tent; Ian names him indispensable aide.
### Scene 3
During raid, enemy illusion technique makes Ian see Jay as old traitor for one fatal second.
### Scene 4
Ian hesitates, Jay is struck by revenant claw; Ian erupts, annihilates wave at severe spiritual cost.
### Scene 5
After battle, Ian confesses partial truth and begs forgiveness; Jay, in pain, says he never blamed Ian.

## Chapter 16 - Threads of Trust
### Scene 1
Jay heals slowly; Ian personally handles his chores in secret, pretending it's routine inspections.
### Scene 2
Jay catches Ian doing laundry charms wrong and gently corrects him; both share rare laughter.
### Scene 3
They resume dawn training together: Ian adjusts Jay's sword angles with careful, respectful instruction.
### Scene 4
Luc notices changed atmosphere and jokingly calls them "the frost lord and his sunbeam clerk."
### Scene 5
Night rooftop scene: Jay tells Ian that strength without kindness is hollow, and Ian is both strong and kind.

## Chapter 17 - Summer Retreat at Azure Coast
### Scene 1
Clan retreat to coastal cultivation grounds for morale and cross-discipline exercises.
### Scene 2
Housing shortage forces Ian and Jay into shared two-room pavilion; both are painfully formal.
### Scene 3
Day drills include spirit-current diving; Jay panics underwater, Ian anchors him until calm returns.
### Scene 4
Bonfire evening: Leo and Luc tease openly; Jay defends Ian's dignity while Ian quietly watches, moved.
### Scene 5
Thunderstorm knocks out wards at midnight; Ian and Jay fight side by side against tide-borne specters.

## Chapter 18 - Moonlit Relics
### Scene 1
Treasure-hunt exercise uncovers genuine ancient relic compass reacting to Ian and Jay's paired qi.
### Scene 2
Elders demand relic surrender; rival envoys attempt theft during formal banquet.
### Scene 3
Jay uses clever decoy boxes and servant routes to misdirect thieves; Luc runs interference.
### Scene 4
Ian duels envoy captain on moon bridge, wins narrowly, and secures diplomatic concession.
### Scene 5
Back in pavilion, Jay disinfects Ian's wounds; intimate quiet leaves both shaken by unspoken longing.

## Chapter 19 - Return and Reckoning
### Scene 1
Back at Qingsong, evidence from poison ledgers plus relic theft reveals coordinated campaign targeting Ian specifically.
### Scene 2
Council debates whether Ian's presence endangers clan; whispers suggest expelling him again "for safety."
### Scene 3
Jay speaks before elders despite low rank, testifying to Ian's integrity and sacrifices in recent battles.
### Scene 4
An enemy strike team breaches council hall mid-hearing; Ian and Jay defend elders in brutal close combat.
### Scene 5
Attack repelled. Council keeps Ian and elevates Jay to formal inner-court position; Ian looks devastated, not proud, remembering how he first treated Jay.

## Chapter 20 - Vows Beneath Broken Stars
### Scene 1
Aftermath week: funerals, memorial tablets, rebuilding wards; Ian and Jay work in exhausting tandem.
### Scene 2
Ian offers Jay transfer to prestigious strategist track, thinking distance may protect him from future danger.
### Scene 3
Jay refuses and asks to remain at Ian's side, saying "my path is where I can protect you too."
### Scene 4
Night patrol at ruined watchtower: Ian finally confesses his fear, guilt, and how early coldness still shames him.
### Scene 5
Jay answers with gentle certainty: they are both wounded, both still standing, and can choose each other step by step; book closes on shared vow to face the coming war together.

## Seeding for Book Two
- Coalition politics after Chapter 19 attack.
- Full identity of the original traitor's surviving network.
- Jay's unknown paternal lineage becoming relevant to high-level cultivation inheritance.
- Emotional state at end: explicitly devoted, not yet fully confessed in romantic terms, but bond now undeniable.
